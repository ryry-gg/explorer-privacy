iXplor - Device Sensor Data (iOS)

iXplor records GPS tracks enriched with your iPhone's sensors — barometer, motion, activity, steps, battery — stores everything on your device, and lets you export it anywhere you choose.

Need help or found a bug?
Email james.bolderson@gmail.com and include your iOS version and what you were doing when the problem occurred.

Common questions

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you export it yourself. See the privacy policy.

Why does the app ask for "Always" location access?
So recording can continue while your screen is locked or you're using another app. Recording only happens after you tap Start Tracking on the Dashboard.

How do I record hands-free with Siri?
While tracking, say "Log a POI in iXplor" (or "Mark this location in iXplor"). The full phrase list is in the app under Help.

How do I get my data out?
Open Export in the sidebar — share a ZIP of CSV or JSONL via Files/AirDrop/Mail, or send it to your own HTTP server. Field-by-field schemas are documented in the app under Help → Data Reference.

How do I delete data?
Data → Manage Sessions lets you preview and delete individual sessions. Deleting the app removes everything.

In-app documentation

The app includes a searchable Help screen (open the sidebar → Help) covering every feature, the Siri phrases, and complete database/export schema references.
